Personal page of Alexey A. Mitsyuk

back to main page

Alex is an associate professor at the Faculty of Computer Science of HSE University (Moscow, Russia). He leads the joint department with JetBrains of the School of Software Engineering. He is also a part-time senior research fellow at the Laboratory of Process-Aware Information Systems (PAIS Lab) of the same faculty.

Research Interests

Computer Science; Algorithms; Petri Nets; Information Systems; Software Architecture; Process Analytics; Process Mining; Visualisation and Visual Analytics
