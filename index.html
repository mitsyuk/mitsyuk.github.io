Personal page of Alexey A. Mitsyuk

Alexey is a researcher at PAIS Lab of the Faculty of Computer Science of Higher School of Economics, Moscow.
He is also a senior lecturer.

Projects

Alex involved in several reseach/applied projects of PAIS Lab. He is most active in the following ones:

  • Gena: here the goal is to develop methods to simulate process models and generate artificial event logs with suitable properties.
  • Iskra: here the methods to repair process models according to event logs are developed.
  • Carassius: this is just a simple petri net (.pnml) editor.

Alex has been also involved in more practical projects. Links to some of them are below:

  • Stack trace to UML is an IntelliJ IDEA plug-in that draws UML sequence and communication diagrams from JVM exception stacktraces. The tool has been developed during a bachelor thesis project by Lev Klochkov under the supervision of A. Mitsyuk.
  • The first versions of mobile applications for the Stepik.org (this is a MOOC-constructor and an online-education platform) have been developed by Kirill Makarov and Alexander Karpov as their bachelor thesis projects with supervision of A. Mitsyuk.
